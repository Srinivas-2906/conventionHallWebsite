About Grand Convention

Discover our story and what makes us the premier choice for events of all sizes.

A Legacy of Excellence Since 1995

Grand Convention Center has been the cornerstone of exceptional events for over 25 years. What began as a modest venue has evolved into a premier destination for conferences, weddings, galas, and corporate events.

Our commitment to excellence, attention to detail, and dedication to customer satisfaction have made us the preferred choice for event planners and individuals seeking a memorable venue.

With over 50,000 square feet of versatile event space, cutting-edge technology, and a team of experienced professionals, we provide everything you need to make your event a resounding success.
