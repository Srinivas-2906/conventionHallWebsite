About Grand Horizon

Grand Horizon Convention Center is a premier event venue located in the heart of the city, offering versatile spaces for all types of gatherings. Since our establishment in 2010, we have been the venue of choice for prestigious events, from corporate conferences to elegant weddings.

Our commitment to excellence is reflected in our state-of-the-art facilities, exceptional service, and attention to detail. We understand that every event is unique, which is why our experienced team works closely with clients to customize our spaces and services to meet their specific needs.

With over 50,000 square feet of flexible event space, cutting-edge technology, and world-class amenities, Grand Horizon is designed to elevate your events to new heights.
